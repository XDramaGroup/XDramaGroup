## 👋 Welcome to XDramaGroup
We are the open-source team behind **X Drama** — the world's first Web3 short drama platform built on the "Watch-to-Earn" model. Our mission is to democratize entertainment by letting users access premium short dramas for FREE while earning tangible, deflationary token rewards for their time and attention.

We launch first in **India**, followed by a phased global expansion into Southeast Asia, Japan, Korea, and Mainland China.

## ✨ Core Ecosystem Highlights
- **100% Free Short Dramas**: No paywalls, no subscriptions, no hidden costs.
- **Watch-to-Earn Mechanism**: Earn X Drama Tickets by watching, checking in, sharing content, or inviting friends.
- **XDT Native Token**: Tickets are converted to XDT, our deflationary utility token with a hard cap of 1B total supply, converging to 100M in final circulation.
- **8-Layer Deflationary Model**: Ensures long-term scarcity and value appreciation for XDT holders.
- **Open & Transparent**: All core rules are open-sourced, with on-chain transparency for tokenomics and contributions.
